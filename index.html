About SAITM

St. Andrews Institute of Technology and Management (SAITM), located in Gurugram, Haryana, is a leading institute offering top-quality education in engineering, management, and computer applications. It focuses on skill development, innovation, and research, with a goal to produce industry-ready professionals. The campus provides modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and excellent placement opportunities for its students.
